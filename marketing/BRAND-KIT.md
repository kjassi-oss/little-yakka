# Little Yakka — Social Brand Kit

One-stop reference for setting up and running Facebook, Instagram and X.
All images referenced here live in this `marketing/` folder.

---

## 1. Brand foundations

**Product one-liner (primary hook):**
> The chore app your kids will ask to open

**Official tagline (on the logo):**
> Rewarding positive lifelong habits

**Supporting lines (rotate in captions):**
- Stars, streaks & a weekly Bonus Wheel — chores, but fun.
- 100% free • No ads • Kids never sign in
- Built by an Aussie dad of three 🇦🇺
- "Yakka" is Aussie for hard work. Little Yakka is the fun kind.

**Colours:**
| Use | Colour | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary indigo | headings, borders, night-sky backgrounds | `#334487` |
| Star yellow | highlights, CTA pills, stars | `#FDE047` |
| Amber (pop word) | emphasis word in headlines | `#F59E0B` |
| Rainbow accents | confetti, icons — pink / blue / green / orange / purple | `#EC4899` `#3B82F6` `#22C55E` `#F97316` `#8B5CF6` |
| Muted indigo | sublines / secondary text | `#5A628C` |
| Background | white | `#FFFFFF` |

**Type:** graphics use a heavy rounded sans (Segoe UI Black in the rendered
assets; Baloo 2 or Nunito are free lookalikes in Canva/CapCut).

**Voice:** warm, playful, Aussie. Talk like a mate who's great with kids —
never corporate. Australian spelling always. Never show real kids' faces or
names; screenshots use demo data only. No behavioural/medical claims — we say
"makes chores fun" and "builds habits", nothing stronger.

**Hashtag pool (pick 5–8 per post, rotate):**
`#chorechart #kidschores #starchart #pocketmoney #kidsroutine #parentingtips
#parentinghacks #ausparents #aussiemums #aussiedads #mumlife #dadlife
#familyapp #positiveparenting #kidsrewards`

## 6. Channel roles (from the launch strategy)

- **Instagram** — home base; all original reels/carousels made here first
- **Facebook page** — trust signal + free cross-post target
- **Facebook groups** — participation as Kiran-the-dad, not the brand
- **TikTok** — repost-only for first 60 days
- **X** — presence + builder-community posts; low effort, cross-post only
- **Reddit** — launch-moment founder posts (r/daddit etc.), then occasional
